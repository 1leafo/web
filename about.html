about leafo

Merrick (born August 4, 2007), also known as leafo, is an 18 year old Filipino Baguio-Based innovative musician known for his versatility and wide-ranging blend of indie, pop, alternative rock, emo-rap, hyperpop, and more. Affiliated with the collective frequency, leafo crafts a unique sound that resonates with listeners and has garnered attention for his heartfelt lyrics and melodic compositions. His music explores vulnerability through emotional lyrics, combining reflective storytelling with communicable energy that invites fans to connect.

As a young and growing artist, leafo’s music has gained traction on every other platform, where his authentic approach to songwriting has attracted a loyal following. With each release, he continues to elevate his sound and connect with listeners on a deep level. His ability to display complex emotions through simple yet profound lyrics has made him a favorite among fans of alternative music.
